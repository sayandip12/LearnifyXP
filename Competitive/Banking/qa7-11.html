Quantitative Aptitude Quiz - Time & Work

Earn 0.5 coins for each correct answer!

1. If A can complete a work in 10 days and B can complete the same work in 15 days, how long will they take to complete the work together?

2. If 12 men can complete a work in 18 days, how many days will 8 men take to complete the same work?

3. A is twice as efficient as B. If B can complete a work in 30 days, how long will they take to complete the work together?

4. If 5 women can complete a work in 8 days working 6 hours a day, how many days will 10 women take to complete the same work working 4 hours a day?

5. A can do a work in 20 days. After working for 5 days, he is joined by B and they finish the remaining work in 6 days. How many days would B alone take to complete the work?

6. A and B can complete a work in 12 days, B and C in 15 days, and A and C in 20 days. How long will A, B, and C take together to complete the work?

7. A can complete 1/4th of a work in 5 days. How many days will he take to complete the whole work?

8. If 6 men and 8 women can complete a work in 10 days, and 3 men and 12 women can complete the same work in 8 days, how long will 12 men take to complete the work?

9. A can complete a work in 12 days. B is 50% more efficient than A. How long will B take to complete the same work?

10. A and B can complete a work in 15 days, B and C in 20 days, and C and A in 12 days. How long will A alone take to complete the work?

11. A can complete a work in 20 days. After working for 4 days, he is joined by B and they finish the remaining work in 8 days. How many days would B alone take to complete the work?

12. If 3 men or 4 women can complete a work in 60 days, how long will 6 men and 8 women take to complete the same work?

13. A can complete a work in 10 days and B in 15 days. They work together for 4 days, then B leaves. How many more days will A take to complete the remaining work?

14. A is twice as efficient as B. If they work together, they can complete a work in 12 days. How long will B alone take to complete the work?

15. 20 men can complete a work in 15 days working 8 hours a day. How many men are needed to complete the work in 10 days working 12 hours a day?

16. A can complete a work in 12 days, B in 18 days, and C in 24 days. They start working together but A leaves after 4 days and B leaves 3 days before completion. How many days did the work take?

17. 5 men and 2 boys can complete a work in 10 days, while 3 men and 4 boys can complete it in 12 days. In how many days can 2 men and 3 boys complete the work?

18. A can complete a work in 20 days. B is 25% more efficient than A. How long will they take to complete the work together?

19. A can complete a work in 15 days and B in 20 days. They work together for 5 days, then both leave. The remaining work is completed by C in 5 days. How long would C alone take to complete the whole work?

20. 12 men can complete a work in 18 days. After 6 days, 4 more men joined them. How many days will they take to complete the remaining work?
